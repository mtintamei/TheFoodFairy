Making Food Distribution Simple

Food Fairy connects surplus food from restaurants and grocery stores with local communities in need. Our efficient distribution system ensures that good food feeds people, not landfills. Through our network of volunteers and partners, we're creating a more sustainable and equitable food system.

Food Recovery

We collect surplus food from our partner restaurants, grocery stores, and farms, ensuring good food doesn't go to waste.

Quality Check

Our trained volunteers inspect and sort all donations to ensure they meet our high food safety standards.

Distribution Planning

We map out the most efficient delivery routes to community centers, shelters, and families in need.

Same-Day Delivery

Our volunteer drivers ensure fresh food reaches those who need it most, all within the same day of collection.
