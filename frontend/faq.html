Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate food? ▼
Contact us at hello@foodfairy.org or call +1 (555) 000-0000. We accept fresh produce, packaged non-perishable goods, and prepared meals from restaurants. All donations must meet our food safety standards.
How do I become a volunteer? ▼
Apply online through our website. We require a background check, and volunteers must be at least 18 years old. We offer training for various roles including food collection, distribution, and administrative support.
What areas do you serve? ▼
Our primary service area is Brooklyn, NY, with plans to expand to surrounding boroughs. We focus on high-need communities to maximize our impact.
How are donations used? ▼
100% of donations go directly to food redistribution. We collect surplus food, ensure its quality, and distribute it to local families, shelters, and community centers within 24 hours of collection.
